Explore 5 Scenarios of the HFDS Below

(There may be performance issues currently, which is being worked on)

Scenario 1

Simulate paying attention to the road and not paying attention by clicking the corresponding buttons. For either choice to work, HFD must first be activated. Not paying attention for long enough will cause you to go through each warning stage.(Note: If you fail to pay attention after all 3 warnings, HFD will turn off and you will not be allowed to reactivate it.)

Scenario 2

Grab the wheel with your mouse to turn it left and right. While turning, HFD will relinquish control to the driver, which is signified by the blue light.
