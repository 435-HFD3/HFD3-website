CSE435: Hands Free Driving - Group 3

Project Overview

The HFDS system allows a vehicle to automatically steer, accelerate and brake in certain highway conditions. Once activated, the driver can remove their hands from the wheel and is continuously monitored to ensure active engagement with the road. Warnings are issued if 1) the system needs the driver to reclaim control, 2) The driver is deemed distracted.
